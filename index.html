ABOUT US

El Cafetal is a family-owned business specializing in the sale of ground coffee, offering both traditional coffee and special preparations like the dreamy affogato. With a focus on high-quality products and preserving the essence of traditional coffee-making methods, El Cafetal provides customers with a unique and authentic coffee experience that blends rich flavors with time-honored techniques.

COMMON CUSTOMER QUESTIONS

What types of coffee do you offer at El Cafetal?.

At El Cafetal, we offer a variety of options including ground coffee, traditional brewed coffee, and special preparations like our signature affogato. We take pride in providing high-quality coffee that captures the essence of traditional coffee-making.
